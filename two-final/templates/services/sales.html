Different Sales-force Testing Service TestSync Global offer

There are various Sales-force testing services available in the market, catering to different needs of organizations. Some of the different Sales-force testing services offered by us are:

Unit Testing

A software testing technique in which individual units or components of a program are tested in isolation from the rest of the system to ensure their correctness and functionality.

System Testing

A type of software testing that evaluates the overall performance and functionality of a system or application in its entirety, ensuring it meets the requirements and specifications.

User Acceptance Testing

The final phase of testing that involves end-users or clients verifying the system to ensure it meets their requirements and is ready for deployment.

Production Testing

Testing performed on a live production system to ensure its stability, reliability, and performance while minimizing any impact on end-users.

Salesforce Load Testing

Testing the performance and scalability of a Salesforce application by simulating multiple users simultaneously accessing the system under various load conditions.

Exploratory Testing

A testing approach where testers actively explore a system or application, design and execute tests on the fly to find defects and verify behavior, and gain a better understanding of the product.
