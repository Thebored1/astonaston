Different CX Testing Service TestSync Global offer

TestSync Global offers several different CX testing services to help organizations improve their customer experience. Each of these services is designed to help organizations identify areas where they can improve their customer experience and make the necessary changes to improve customer satisfaction.

Usability Testing

Usability testing is a process used to assess the usability of a product, usually through feedback from users.

Customer Journey Mapping

Customer journey mapping is a process used to understand the experience a customer has with a company.

AB Testing

AB testing is a statistical method used to compare two or more variants of a product, usually a website or app, to see which one performs better.

Digital Personalization

Digital personalization is the process of customizing a user's digital experience to their specific needs and preferences.

Localization Testing

Localization testing is the process of testing how well a product or service functions when adapted to specific markets.

Accessibility Testing

Accessibility testing is the process of ensuring that a website or application is accessible to all users, regardless of their abilities or disabilities.
