ABOUT US.

TestSync Global is an independent software testing and QA company and was founded with the vision of delivering high-quality solutions in accordance with current market trends. At TestSync Global "We believe in providing Quality output". We follow best Quality Assurance processes during Product Life Cycle for delivering high quality outputs. Our services include all the trending environments like iOS, android, windows, mac users.

We provide end to end testing for our customers and offer Functional, System, Automation, Performance, Security, API, Unit tests across Web/Desktop and Mobile software.

Our specialised testing services include Mobile, Website, Performance, cloud migration, enterprise software testing, RPA testing, AI, Cloud testing, IoT testing. TestSync Global is your one-stop partner for all of your testing requirements.

Our world class test infrastructure helped more than 15+ product companies in India, USA, Canada, Europe, Singapore and the UK.

TestSync Global has proven expertise in Software Testing. Our vertical experience is in Finance, Gaming, Travel, Aviation, E-Commerce, ELearning, CRM, Health domain application software.
